Flowify Rap Game Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

English

Flowify Rap Game is a freestyle rap practice application developed by Mamba Apps.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Apple, we may receive your Apple account identifier, email address if shared, and name if Apple provides it during the first sign-in.

We may store basic account information such as display name, account creation date, last sign-in date, freestyle session count, and video creation count.

Authentication and Data Storage

We use Firebase Authentication for Apple Sign In and Firebase Firestore to store basic account information and app statistics.

Subscriptions

Premium subscriptions are processed through Apple In-App Purchase. Subscription status is managed using RevenueCat. We do not collect or store your payment card information.

Camera, Microphone and Photos

Flowify Rap Game may request access to your camera and microphone to record freestyle videos. Photo library access may be requested to save exported videos. Recordings are processed locally on your device and are not automatically uploaded to our servers.

Third-Party Services

Flowify Rap Game may use Apple, Firebase, and RevenueCat to provide authentication, subscriptions, and app functionality.

Children's Privacy

Flowify Rap Game is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your account data by contacting us at info@mambaapps.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.

Topladığımız Bilgiler

Apple ile giriş için Firebase Authentication, temel hesap bilgileri ve uygulama istatistiklerini saklamak için Firebase Firestore kullanıyoruz.

Üçüncü Taraf Servisler
